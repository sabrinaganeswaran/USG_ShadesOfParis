ABOUT MICHAEL KORS

Michael Kors (born Karl Anderson, Jr.; August 9, 1959)[3] is a New York City-based fashion designer of American sportswear. Kors was the first women’s ready-to-wear designer for the French house Celine, from 1997 to 2003 Products are sold throughout its portfolio of retail stores worldwide, as well as through its online store via www.michaelkors.com
